Stock vs BNMV

Precision

FFT Analysis

Unlike stock implementations that can feel semi-random, BNMV uses direct mathematical analysis (Fast Fourier Transform) to ensure every light corresponds exactly to a frequency range.

Technical Docs →
Smoothness

60 FPS & 12-Bit

We analyze frequencies at 60 FPS with 4096 light levels (12-bit depth). Stock implementations are often limited to 20 FPS and 3 light levels (~2-bit).

See Devices →
Versatility

Multi-Source

Capture audio via Media Projection, Microphone, or Shizuku. Works with Spotify, YouTube Music, and even games with low latency (down to 20ms).

Hardware Support

Nothing Glyph Interface Native Support

Fully optimized for Phone (1), Phone (2), Phone (2a), Phone (2a Plus), and Phone (3). Every glyph segment and sub-zone is used and controlled independently via customizable presets.


Not on a Nothing Phone? No problem.

Universal compatibility: Every Android phone is compatible with the Haptic motor and Flashlight visualization modes. BNMV is NOT locked to Nothing hardware.

The Philosophy

◉

Deterministic Sync

No randomness. No "guessing" the beat. Every flash is a direct result of mathematical audio analysis.

◈

Open & Customizable

Controlled by `zones.config`. Tweak existing presets or create your own to support new devices or styles.

❖

Privacy First

Audio is processed locally on-device. No audio data is stored or transmitted. Total transparency.
